Squawk Talk is a self intiated podcast I created with a friend. We both wanted to talk about topics in relationd to art school, and more sepcifically, to our classes. We recorded each session once a week and eventually came out with a series!

During my research stage I found many podcast websites tended to follow a similar layout. I planned for the layout to draw the user into the main content of the site, the episodes, while remaining fresh
