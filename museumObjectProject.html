For this project I was tasked with bringing a famouse piece of art into the digital realm. The goal was to juxtapose traditional ways of artmaking with the interactive elements on the web. I chose a still life by Georges Braque as my case study. During the project
I made my own still life inspired by Braque's painting. Then, I digitized pieces of my original still life and put them in motion. The project as a whole utalizes the internet's capacity for movement. As such the painting is in a perpetual state of motion so long as the user is viewing the webpage.
