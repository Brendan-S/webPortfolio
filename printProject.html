Print Project

For this project I was tasked with creating a printed piece that reveales a hidden aspect of itself. An additional constraint plased upon me was to utalise a risograph printer. My goal when designing the piece was to induce a slower pace of reading. On one side are photographs of me that that unfold in an uncanny manner. This causes the viewer to slow down and read the object at a slower pace due to the disorienting layout.
