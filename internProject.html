Precision Effect

During the summer of 2018 I had the opportunity of assisting Precisioneffect with a branding project. The goal was to create a campaign that informs college age students about the health care industry.

The images shown are some of the tactics created for the campaign. On the project I took the role of studio artist and assisted the creative team in their efforts. I brainstormed with the team and helped ideate some of the campaign tactics and their executions on social media. I also assisted other studio artists in their own projects, and daily tasks. At the end of the internship I helped present the different tactics, and their executions in a company wide meeting along with my fellow team mates.
